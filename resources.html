💡Reading Tips for Parents

Build a love for reading through simple, joyful habits.

📖

Read together daily

Even 10 minutes a day makes a difference.

🧒📚

Let them choose

Support their interests — whether it's fantasy, animals, or comics.

🛋️

Create a cozy reading spot

Make reading time feel special and inviting.

❓

Ask questions

Talk about the story to build comprehension and curiosity.

👀📘

Be a reading role model

Let them see you reading too!

📅

Make Thursdays your book day!

Join our TYRC and turn it into a weekly tradition!

🧰 Recommended Tools

Help support your child's reading journey with these helpful resources:

  • Epic! — A digital library for kids under 12.
  • Libby — Borrow eBooks/audiobooks from your local library.
  • Storyline Online — Celebrities read aloud with visuals.
  • PBS Kids Games — Fun and educational games for literacy.
  • Book Creator — Kids can create their own digital books.

Don't forget to check out our TYRC every Thursday for curated stories and questions!
